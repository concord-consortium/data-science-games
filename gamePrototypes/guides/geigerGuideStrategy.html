Strategy Hints

The problem in this game is that if you get exposed to too much radiation, you lose. So you need some combination of:

  • Getting less exposure with each measurement.
  • Taking fewer measurements.

So think about these issues. For example, ask,

Why do you get more exposure sometimes and less at others? How could you use fewer measurements to figure out where the source is?

Most people find the first question easier.

Other things to consider:

  • What other kinds of graphs can you make?
