The Game

A tiny amount of a dangerous radioactive substance was dropped somewhere in the dark gray-green region in the middle of your lab.

Position your radiation detector (it shows up as a diamond in the field) by (a) clicking or (b) typing values into the coordinate boxes.

Click the measure/collect button. When you do,

  • If you are close enough to the source, you collect it and win.
  • Otherwise, you learn the strength of the radiation there (in counts) and accumulate exposure ("Dose").

CODAP records the x and y coordinates of your measurements as well as the counts.

Use data to help you figure out where the speck is.
